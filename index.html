Our Mission

At Igniting Minds, we believe every child has unique potential waiting to be unlocked. Our after school program provides a stimulating environment where children can explore, create, and grow beyond the traditional classroom.

Founded by experienced educators, our program combines academic support with creative activities to foster well-rounded development. We focus on building confidence, curiosity, and critical thinking skills that will serve children throughout their lives.

Our dedicated team of mentors and teachers are passionate about helping each child discover their strengths and overcome challenges in a supportive, nurturing atmosphere.

World Scholars Cup Preparation

Specialized training for academic excellence and global competition

Excel in Global Academic Competitions

Our specialized World Scholars Cup program prepares students to excel in this prestigious global academic competition. We provide comprehensive training in all areas of the competition:

  • Collaborative Writing strategies and techniques
  • Scholar's Challenge subject mastery
  • Team Debate preparation and practice
  • Curriculum-aligned subject knowledge building
  • Mock tournaments and practice sessions
  • Critical thinking and rapid learning skills

Our experienced coaches have a proven track record of helping students achieve top honors at regional and global rounds of the World Scholars Cup.
